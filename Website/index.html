Live Primitive is the world's first hunter-gatherer resort. Situated in the forests of North America, you will experience life as a member of a hunter-gatherer tribe made up of fellow guests and our experienced guides.

To join a tribe, first pick your role.

  • Hunter
  • Gatherer

As a hunter you are responsible for providing meat and pelts for your tribe.

You will participate in the following activities:

  • Tracking
  • Spearing
  • Butchering
  • Trapping

In order to hunt effectively, you will learn how to track wild animals.

This will include analysing foot prints and disturbances in terrain.

You will learn the proper technique for hunting with a spear.

This includes both close-distance attack and long-range spear throwing.

Once you have made a kill, you will learn how to correctly butcher the animal in order to transport the meat back to your tribe.

You will use specialised tools crafted by the gatherers of your tribe in order to butcher the animal.

You will learn how to build and set traps for small animals.

This will include rabbit traps and fish traps.

As a gatherer you are responsible for managing the resources of your tribe.

You will participate in the following activities:

  • Foraging
  • Cooking
  • Crafting
  • Weaving

You will learn how to forage for important ingredients to use in cooking.

You will also search for material that will be useful for crafting and weaving.

You will learn the ancient cooking techniques of our ancestors!

You will learn the ins and outs of cooking on an open fire and managing the charcoals.

You will also learn how to use ancient cooking equipment

You will learn how to craft tools useful for hunting, cooking and weaving.

You will also learn how to build a shelter for your tribe to sleep under.

You will learn how to use thread and needle to create basic clothing.

You will also use these skills to make blankets and mats.

You will also create nets for fishing.
